ABOUT ME

My name is Kate and I have been around animals all of my life. I have 2 dogs Leffe and Maise, who have both been with me since their lives began. You will find Leffe sleeping a lot and Maisie (nick named Crazy Maisie) being just that! Maisie will most certainly be accompanying us on the majority of our adventures, whilst Leffe may well decide to sleep a little bit more!

Setting up my own business involving animals has always been a dream of mine and with my love of animals and the great outdoors Kate’s Furbabies seemed like the perfect way to combine the two!

We offer a range of dog walking services to accommodate you and your doggie, whether you want one walk per day or two, if you would prefer your doggie to be walked on its own for that extra bit of attention or with playmates, if you just want us to nip in to allow them to relieve themselves, feed them, play with your pooch or to simply just keep them company.  Anything to keep your pooch at their happiest!
