OUR ETHOS

We believe in treating every dog as if they were our own, which means keeping them safe and keeping them active and giving them the attention that they require.

Furbabies is based in Crowborough, East Susses, the perfect location to give your doggies the greatest opportunity for a really good run around, either in local country parks or the stunning Ashdown Forest.

Our service is offered throughout Crowborough and surrounding areas. (We are willing to travel further afield, but for more information please contact us) We are DBS registered, as well as being registered with the National Association of Pet Sitters and Dog Walkers (NARPS), and fully insured

All animals must be up to date with worming, flea/tick treatments, vaccinations and insurance. Please note we are not liable for any vet bills and/or 3rd party claims.
